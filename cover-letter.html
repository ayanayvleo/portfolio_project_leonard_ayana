Cover Letter

Dear Hiring Manager,

I am writing to express my interest in an entry-level software engineering opportunity. I bring a strong foundation of discipline and technical troubleshooting through my background in the U.S. Army and my continued education in software engineering.

I approach work with structure, ownership, and curiosity. My goal is to contribute to a team that values clean solutions and continuous improvement. I enjoy debugging because it turns confusion into clarity.

In recent projects, I practiced HTML, CSS, and JavaScript by building a multi-page website with reusable styling, navigation, and interactive scripting. I also prioritize clear writing and professional formatting, because good communication prevents mistakes.

I would welcome the chance to discuss how my technical mindset and reliability can support your team. Thank you for your time and consideration.

Sincerely,
Ayana Leonard
